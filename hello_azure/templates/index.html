Voice-Based Chat GPT Support and Guide Document

Introduction

Welcome to our voice-based chat GPT powered by Azure OpenAI! This document serves as a comprehensive guide to help you navigate and utilize the features of our chatbot effectively.

Getting Started

To access the voice-based chat GPT, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit our web application
  2. Click on the mike icon
  3. Start a conversation by typing or speaking your query.

Basic Commands

You can interact with our chatbot using the following basic commands:

  • "Hello" or "Hi": Start a conversation with the chatbot.
  • "What can you do?": Learn about the capabilities of the chatbot.
  • "Help": Get assistance with using the chatbot.
  • "Goodbye" or "Exit": End the conversation with the chatbot.

Advanced Features

Our chatbot offers several advanced features to enhance your experience:

Natural Language Understanding: The chatbot can understand and respond to complex queries in natural language.

Contextual Understanding:It maintains context across conversations, allowing for more coherent interactions.

Multimodal Input:You can input text or speak your queries using voice commands.

Personalization: The chatbot can learn from user interactions to provide personalized responses over time.

Troubleshooting

Encountering issues with the chatbot? Here are some common troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Refresh the chat interface.
  • Try using different browsers or devices.
  • Contactour technical support team for further assistance at support@example.com.

Best Practices

To make the most out of your interactions with the chatbot, consider the following best practices:

  • Speak clearly and enunciate your words when using voice commands.
  • Provide specific details when asking questions to receive accurate responses.
  • Use natural language and avoid complex jargon to improve understanding.
  • Provide feedback on the chatbot's responses to help us improve its performance.
